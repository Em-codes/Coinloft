How Does It Work?

It's so easy to get started on Coinloft's Digital Assets & Trade Investments

Create an account

Register an account, verify your email and fund your wallet with of the supported currencies. Don't know where to get BTC or ETH? Our support staff can guide you through!

Make a trade investment

Select any coin of your choice or coin you possess and invest with a plan that suits you.

Withdraw/Rollover investment

Daily, your interest will be credited to your investment portfolio. Upon maturity, your capital and interest will be automatically credited to your wallet from where you can make instant withdrawal!
